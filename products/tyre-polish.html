FlexFive Tyre Polish

Shine That Turns Heads!

FlexFive Tyre Polish is specially formulated to restore that deep black showroom shine to your tyres. It creates a protective barrier that resists dust, grime, and UV rays—keeping your tyres cleaner and shinier for longer.

Easy to apply with a spray-and-wipe method, suitable for all tyre types, and leaves no sticky residue.

Usage Instructions

  • Wash and dry tyres completely before applying
  • Shake bottle well
  • Spray evenly on tyre sidewalls
  • Allow 1-2 minutes to absorb
  • Wipe off excess with microfiber cloth
  • Do not apply to tread or braking surfaces
