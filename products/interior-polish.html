Usage Instructions

  • Shake the bottle well before use
  • Spray a small amount of polish directly on the dashboard or interior surface
  • Spread evenly using a soft microfiber cloth
  • Buff gently to achieve a long-lasting shine
  • Avoid applying on steering wheel, gear knob, or pedals for safety
  • Repeat application every 2–3 weeks for best protection
  • Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight
